Hello, I'm @thebbqingbrit

With over 8 years of experience in wedding videography and a growing passion for photography, I specialise in creating timeless, natural films and photographs that tell your unique story. My approach is relaxed and unobtrusive, allowing genuine moments to unfold naturally.

Based in the heart of the British countryside, I travel throughout the UK to capture your special moments. Whether it's an intimate elopement or a grand celebration, I'm passionate about preserving the emotions and details that make your day uniquely yours.

When I'm not behind the camera or editing, you'll find me exploring new locations, firing up the barbecue, or planning the next adventure with my gear in tow.
